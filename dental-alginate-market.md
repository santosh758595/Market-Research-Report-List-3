<p><p>Dental alginate is a key material in dentistry, used for making impressions of patients' teeth. There are three main types of alginate used in dental applications: Sodium Alginate, Potassium Alginate, and Ammonium Alginate. Sodium Alginate is commonly used due to its good compatibility with gypsum materials. Potassium Alginate is used in high-precision dental impressions. Ammonium Alginate is preferred for its longer working and setting times, making it suitable for complex dental impressions. Each type offers unique properties and benefits in the dental alginate market.</p></p>
