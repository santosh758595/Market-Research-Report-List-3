<p><p>The Tungsten Copper Rod market offers three main types based on surface finishing - Sintered Surface, Forged Surface, and Ground Surface. Sintered Surface rods are processed using powder metallurgy ensuring high density and uniformity. Forged Surface rods are shaped through mechanical pressing, resulting in improved strength and durability. Ground Surface rods are finely finished through grinding, achieving precise dimensions and smooth surface texture. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in industries such as aerospace, electronics, and automotive.</p></p>
