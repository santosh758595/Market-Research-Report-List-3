<p><p>Practice Management Systems (PMS) are software applications designed to streamline administrative and financial tasks in medical practices. There are two main market types for PMS - Integrated Practice Management Systems and Standalone Practice Management Systems. Integrated systems are part of a larger Electronic Health Record (EHR) system, offering a more comprehensive solution for healthcare providers. Standalone systems, on the other hand, are independent software solutions focused solely on practice management tasks. Both types aim to improve operational efficiency and enhance patient care delivery in medical practices.</p></p>
