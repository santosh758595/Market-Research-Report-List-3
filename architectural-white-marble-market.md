<p><p>Architectural white marble market can be divided into two main types: natural marble and artificial marble. Natural marble is sourced from quarries and is known for its unique veining and patterns, making it a premium choice for luxury architectural projects. On the other hand, artificial marble is produced using a combination of natural stone particles and resins, offering a more cost-effective alternative with a consistent appearance. Both types cater to different market segments based on budget, aesthetics, and performance requirements.</p></p>
