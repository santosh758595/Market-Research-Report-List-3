<p><p>The CCTV camera market consists of several types of cameras. Dome cameras are round-shaped and provide a wide coverage area, making them suitable for indoor use. Bullet cameras are cylindrical and best suited for outdoor surveillance due to their long-range capabilities. Box cameras are larger in size and offer powerful zooming and focusing abilities, making them ideal for commercial use. PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) cameras can be remotely controlled to pan, tilt, and zoom, allowing for flexible monitoring. These camera types cater to different surveillance needs and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
