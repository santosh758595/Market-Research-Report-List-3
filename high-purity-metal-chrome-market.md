<p><p>High Purity Metal Chrome market is divided into two main types: Metal Chromium Powder and Electrolytic Chromium Powder. Metal Chromium Powder is produced by reducing chromium ore with carbon in an electric furnace, resulting in fine particles of high purity chromium. Electrolytic Chromium Powder is manufactured through electrolysis of chromium chloride, yielding a product with even higher purity levels. Both types are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics for their exceptional corrosion resistance and high heat resistance properties.</p></p>
