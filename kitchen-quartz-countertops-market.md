<p><ul><li>Casting Molding</li><li>Press Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kitchen quartz countertops are available in two main market types: casting molding and press molding. Casting molding involves pouring a mixture of quartz and resin into a mold, allowing it to set and harden into the desired shape. Press molding, on the other hand, involves compressing the quartz mixture using high pressure and heat to create a solid countertop surface. Both methods result in durable and stylish countertops that are highly sought after in the market.</p></p>
