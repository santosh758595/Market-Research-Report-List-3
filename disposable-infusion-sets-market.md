<p><p>Disposable infusion sets are medical devices used to deliver fluids, such as medications or nutrients, into a patient's bloodstream. Pump infusion sets are designed for use with an infusion pump, which delivers a controlled amount of fluid. These sets typically include a pump, a cartridge or bag containing the drug or fluid, and tubing with a needle or catheter. Non-pump infusion sets lack a pump mechanism and are manually operated, usually consisting of a drip chamber, tubing, and a needle or catheter. Both types are sterile, single-use devices that enhance patient safety and convenience.</p></p>
