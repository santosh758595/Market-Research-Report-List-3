<p><p>Solid-phase oligonucleotide synthesis market includes various types such as primers, probes, intermediate-scale synthesis oligos, large-scale synthesis oligos, linkers, and adaptors. Primers are short nucleic acid sequences used for DNA replication, while probes are used in molecular biology to detect specific DNA sequences. Intermediate-scale synthesis oligos are oligonucleotides synthesized on a smaller scale for research purposes, while large-scale synthesis oligos are produced in larger quantities for commercial applications. Linkers and adaptors are used to modify the properties of oligonucleotides for specific applications.</p></p>
