<p><p>The lab disposable centrifuge tube market is classified into two types: Polypropylene (PP) and Polystyrene (PS). Polypropylene tubes are made from a thermoplastic polymer that offers excellent resistance to chemicals, high temperatures, and mechanical stress. They are commonly used for general laboratory applications and are autoclavable. Polystyrene tubes, on the other hand, are made from a versatile synthetic polymer that is transparent and has good clarity. They are often used for short-term biological sample storage and are not autoclavable. Both types cater to different laboratory needs based on their specific properties and applications.</p></p>
