<p><ul><li>Unidirectional</li><li>Two-Way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bullet-proof glass is classified into two main types: unidirectional and two-way. Unidirectional glass is designed to resist projectiles coming from one direction only, commonly used for bank teller windows and secure government buildings. Two-way glass, on the other hand, provides protection against gunfire and shattering from both sides, making it suitable for applications like security vehicles and high-security facilities. Both types of bullet-proof glass offer varying levels of protection and can be customized to meet specific security requirements.</p></p>
