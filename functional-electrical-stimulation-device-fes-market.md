<p><p>A Functional Electrical Stimulation (FES) device is a medical device that uses electrical current to stimulate paralyzed muscles in order to restore function and improve mobility in individuals with neurological and musculoskeletal disorders. FES devices work by delivering electrical impulses to specific muscles or nerve fibers, causing them to contract and produce a desired movement. These devices are commonly used in the treatment of conditions such as spinal cord injuries, stroke, multiple sclerosis, and cerebral palsy.</p><p>The global FES device market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the main factors driving market growth is the increasing prevalence of neurological and musculoskeletal conditions that require FES intervention.
