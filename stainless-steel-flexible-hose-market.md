<p><p>Stainless Steel Flexible Hose Market Types include Strip-wound Hoses and Corrugated Hoses. Strip-wound Hoses are constructed by winding a metal strip spirally around a metal core, providing flexibility and durability. Corrugated Hoses feature a series of ridges or corrugations along the length of the hose, allowing for greater flexibility and movement in various applications. Both types are commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and plumbing for their ability to withstand high temperatures and pressures.</p></p>
