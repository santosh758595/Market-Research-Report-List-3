<p><strong>The Galvanized Round Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Galvanized Round Steel</li><li>Cold Pull Galvanized Round Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Galvanized Round Steel comes in two main types: Hot Rolled Galvanized Round Steel and Cold Pull Galvanized Round Steel. Hot Rolled Galvanized Round Steel is produced by heating steel ingots until they are malleable and shaping them into round tubes using rollers. Cold Pull Galvanized Round Steel, on the other hand, is made by pulling steel through a die at room temperature to create the desired shape. Both types of Galvanized Round Steel are commonly used in construction, manufacturing, and other industries for their durability and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
