<p><ul><li>Alkaryl Sulfonates</li><li>Sodium Sulfonate</li><li>Calcium Sulfonate</li><li>Magnesium Sulfonate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sulfonate additives are classified into various types based on the cation they are associated with. Alkaryl Sulfonates are commonly used in lubricant additives and fuel additives due to their high thermal stability. Sodium Sulfonate is widely used in corrosion inhibitors for its water solubility and excellent rust protection. Calcium Sulfonate is known for its extreme pressure properties and is commonly used in grease and oil additives. Magnesium Sulfonate is used in metalworking fluids for its anti-wear and lubricity properties.</p></p>
