<p><p>Extruded Aluminum Tubing is available in two main types: Seamless Extruded Aluminum Tubing and Ordinary Extruded Aluminum Tubing. Seamless tubing is manufactured without any welding or joints, providing a smooth surface and high strength for demanding applications. On the other hand, Ordinary Extruded Aluminum Tubing is produced with welded seams, offering a cost-effective solution for less critical applications. Both types of tubing are widely used in industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace for their lightweight and corrosion-resistant properties.</p></p>
