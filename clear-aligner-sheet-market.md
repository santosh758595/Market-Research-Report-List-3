<p><p>Clear aligner sheets are available in various market types such as multi-layer TPU, single-layer TPU, PETG, and other materials. Multi-layer TPU sheets consist of multiple layers of thermoplastic polyurethane, providing increased strength and durability. Single-layer TPU sheets are made from a single layer of thermoplastic polyurethane, offering a more flexible and comfortable fit. PETG sheets are made from polyethylene terephthalate glycol, known for their transparency and impact resistance. Other materials used in clear aligner sheets include acrylics, polycarbonates, and nylon.</p></p>
