<p><p>Portable mini washing machines come in two main types: single-tub and twin-tub. Single-tub machines have just one compartment for both washing and spinning, making them compact and easy to use. On the other hand, twin-tub machines have separate compartments for washing and spinning, allowing users to wash and spin dry simultaneously. Single-tub machines are ideal for small spaces and quick washes, while twin-tub machines are better suited for users who need more versatility and efficiency in their laundry routine.</p></p>
