<p><p>Defatted Fish Meal comes in two main types - Flame Dried (FD) Fish Meal and Steam Dried (SD) Fish Meal. FD Fish Meal is produced by heating the fish meal with direct flame, resulting in a high protein content and better digestibility. On the other hand, SD Fish Meal is produced by using steam to remove excess oil and moisture, making it a more cost-effective option. Both types of defatted fish meal are commonly used in animal feed and aquaculture industries for their high protein content and nutritional value.</p></p>
