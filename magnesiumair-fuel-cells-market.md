<p><p>Magnesium-air fuel cells are divided into two main types: rechargeable and non-rechargeable. Rechargeable magnesium-air fuel cells can be recharged by supplying new magnesium to the cell, allowing for multiple uses. On the other hand, non-rechargeable magnesium-air fuel cells cannot be refilled or reused once the magnesium is consumed. These cells are designed for one-time use only. The market for both types of magnesium-air fuel cells caters to different needs and preferences of consumers looking for energy-efficient and environmentally friendly power sources.</p></p>
