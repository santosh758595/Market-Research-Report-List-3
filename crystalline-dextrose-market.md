<p><p>Crystalline Dextrose is available in two main market types: Monohydrate glucose and Anhydrous glucose. Monohydrate glucose contains one molecule of water per glucose molecule, while Anhydrous glucose does not contain any water molecules. Monohydrate glucose is commonly used in food and beverages as a sweetener and texturizer, while Anhydrous glucose is often used in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and other industrial applications. Both forms of crystalline dextrose offer different benefits and functionalities depending on the specific needs of the end product.</p></p>
