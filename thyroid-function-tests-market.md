<p><strong>The Thyroid Function Tests Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TSH Tests</li><li>FT4 Tests</li><li>FT3 Tests</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thyroid function tests are used to assess the health of the thyroid gland and diagnose thyroid disorders. The market for these tests can be divided into three types: TSH tests, FT4 tests, and FT3 tests. TSH tests measure the levels of thyroid-stimulating hormone and are primarily used to detect hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism. FT4 tests measure the levels of free thyroxine, an essential thyroid hormone involved in metabolism, while FT3 tests measure the levels of free triiodothyronine, another important thyroid hormone. These tests help healthcare professionals evaluate thyroid function and determine appropriate treatment.</p></p>
