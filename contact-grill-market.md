<p><ul><li>Liquid Propane (LP) Grills</li><li>Natural Gas (NG) Grills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact grills come in two main types: Liquid Propane (LP) grills and Natural Gas (NG) grills. LP grills are powered by propane gas stored in a tank, offering convenience and portability for outdoor cooking. On the other hand, NG grills are connected to a permanent gas line, providing a more cost-effective and environmentally-friendly option for grilling. Both types of grills offer efficient heat distribution and consistent cooking results, making them popular choices for commercial kitchens and outdoor cooking enthusiasts.</p></p>
