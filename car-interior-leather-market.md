Synthetic leather, on the other hand, is made from synthetic materials like polyurethane and offers a more affordable alternative to genuine leather. While synthetic leather may not have the same premium feel as genuine leather, it is often more versatile and easier to maintain. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and budget constraints in the car interior market.</p></p>
