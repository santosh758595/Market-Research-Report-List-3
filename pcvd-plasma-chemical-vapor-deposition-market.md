There are two main types of PCVD markets: RF Plasma and DC Plasma. RF Plasma utilizes radio frequency energy to create plasma, while DC Plasma uses direct current. Both types have their own advantages and applications, with RF Plasma being more commonly used for high-density plasma processes and DC Plasma being preferred for lower energy consumption and simpler setups.</p></p>
