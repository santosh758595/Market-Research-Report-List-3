<p><p>Portable radiation detectors are essential tools used to detect and measure radiation levels in various environments. The market for these detectors is classified into three main types: Gas Ionization Detectors, Semiconductor Detectors, and Scintillation Detectors. Gas Ionization Detectors rely on the ionization of gas molecules by radiation particles, Semiconductor Detectors use semiconducting materials to measure radiation levels, and Scintillation Detectors detect radiation based on the scintillation of materials when exposed to radiation. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in different applications.</p></p>
