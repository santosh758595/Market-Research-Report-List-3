<p><p>Medical oxygen concentrators and oxygen cylinders are important medical devices used to provide supplemental oxygen to patients who have difficulty breathing or have low oxygen levels in their blood. These devices help deliver high concentrations of oxygen, improving the overall oxygenation of the body.</p><p>Oxygen concentrators are electrical devices that extract oxygen from the air and deliver it to the patient through a nasal cannula or mask. They are commonly used in home care settings as they provide a continuous supply of oxygen and eliminate the need for refilling or changing cylinders. Oxygen cylinders, on the other hand, contain compressed or liquid oxygen and are portable, making them suitable for outdoor or emergency use.</p><p>The global medical oxygen concentrators and oxygen cylinders market is experiencing significant growth.
