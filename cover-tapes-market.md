<p><ul><li>Heat Activated Cover Tapes</li><li>Pressure-sensitive Cover Tapes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cover tapes are used in packaging electronic components on reels. Heat activated cover tapes require a certain amount of heat to adhere to the carrier tape, providing a secure seal. Pressure-sensitive cover tapes, on the other hand, adhere simply by applying pressure, making them more convenient for certain applications. Both types of cover tapes are essential in the electronics industry for protecting sensitive components during shipping and handling, ensuring their integrity and functionality upon arrival.</p></p>
