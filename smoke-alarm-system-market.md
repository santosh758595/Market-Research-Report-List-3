<p><p>The smoke alarm system market includes various types of alarms such as photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric smoke alarms use a light beam to detect smoke particles, while ionization smoke alarms use a chamber filled with ions to detect smoke. Combination smoke alarms combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies for improved accuracy in detecting different types of fires. Each type of smoke alarm system caters to specific needs and preferences of consumers.</p></p>
