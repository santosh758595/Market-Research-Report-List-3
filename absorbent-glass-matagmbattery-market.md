<p><p>An Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) battery is a type of sealed lead-acid battery in which the electrolyte is held in the glass mat separator. This design allows the electrolyte to be absorbed by the mat, making the battery spill-proof and maintenance-free. AGM batteries are commonly used in applications where reliability and long service life are important, such as in vehicles, UPS systems, and renewable energy storage.</p><p>The AGM battery market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6% during the forecast period.
