<p><p>A medical breathing tube is a device used to deliver oxygen or anesthesia to a patient who is unable to breathe on their own. It is inserted into the patient's airway and connected to a breathing apparatus or ventilator. These tubes are commonly used during surgeries, intensive care treatment, or in emergency situations to maintain a patient's airway and ensure adequate oxygenation.</p><p>The medical breathing tube market is expected to witness steady growth in the forecast period. The rising prevalence of respiratory diseases and an increase in the number of surgical procedures are the major factors driving market growth. Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the development of innovative breathing tubes with features like enhanced flexibility, reduced trauma, and improved patient comfort.</p><p>Another significant trend in the market is the growing demand for disposable breathing tubes. These tubes reduce the risk of infection transmission and are preferred in healthcare settings due to their low cost and easy disposal. Moreover, with the increasing adoption of minimally invasive procedures, there is a rising demand for specialized tubes suitable for these techniques.</p><p>Geographically, North America dominates the medical breathing tube market due to a high number of surgical procedures and a well-established healthcare infrastructure.
