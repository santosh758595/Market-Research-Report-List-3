<p><p>Industrial circular connectors are categorized into different types based on their mounting style. Cable mount connectors are directly attached to cables, while flange mount connectors are fixed to a surface using screws. Panel mount connectors are installed into a panel cutout, providing a flush appearance. PCB mount connectors are soldered onto printed circuit boards. Each type of connector serves a specific purpose and is chosen based on the application requirements for secure and reliable electrical connections in industrial settings.</p></p>
