<p><p>The food safety big data market encompasses various types of technologies used to ensure the safety of food products. Barcode technology involves using unique barcodes on food packaging to track and trace products throughout the supply chain. QR code technology utilizes quick response codes that can be scanned by smartphones to provide detailed information about a product's origin, ingredients, and safety. Radio frequency identification technology uses tags to transmit data about a product's location and condition, enabling real-time tracking. Isotope traceability technology uses radioactive isotopes to trace the origin and authenticity of food products.</p></p>
