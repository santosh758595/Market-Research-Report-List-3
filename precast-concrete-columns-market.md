<p><p>The precast concrete columns market can be divided into two main types: frame columns and non-frame columns. Frame columns are typically used in structural frame systems to support vertical loads and provide lateral stability. Non-frame columns, on the other hand, are designed for architectural purposes and do not contribute to the structural support of a building. Both types of columns are essential components in construction projects and play a crucial role in ensuring the overall strength and stability of a structure.</p></p>
