<p><p>Car-sharing market types can be categorized into three main types: P2P (peer-to-peer), station-based, and free-floating. </p><p>In P2P car-sharing, individuals rent out their personal vehicles to others, typically via an online platform. Station-based car-sharing involves designated parking stations where users can pick up and drop off vehicles. Free-floating car-sharing allows users to locate and access available cars through a mobile app, enabling them to pick up and drop off cars anywhere within a defined operating territory. These market types offer different levels of convenience, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness to users.</p></p>
