<p><p>Marine Diesel Oil (MDO) is a type of fuel used in marine engines. It can be categorized into Light Diesel Oil and Heavy-Duty Diesel Oil markets based on its density and viscosity. Light Diesel Oil is a lighter version of MDO with lower density and viscosity, suitable for smaller marine engines. The choice between the two depends on the specific requirements of the engine being used.</p></p>
