<p><ul><li>Wet Mixed Mortar</li><li>Dry Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mortar market consists of two main types: wet mixed mortar and dry mortar. Wet mixed mortar refers to a pre-mixed combination of sand, cement, and water that is ready to use upon delivery. On the other hand, dry mortar is a mixture of sand, cement, additives, and other components that need to be mixed with water on site before application. Both types offer different advantages and are used in various construction applications based on their specific properties and requirements.</p></p>
