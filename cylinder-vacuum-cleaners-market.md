<p><p>Cylinder vacuum cleaners come in two main types: bagged and bagless. Bagged vacuum cleaners use disposable bags to collect dirt and debris, which need to be replaced regularly. On the other hand, bagless vacuum cleaners have a dust container that can be emptied and reused, eliminating the need for disposable bags. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, with bagged models typically being more hygienic and less messy to empty, while bagless models are more cost-effective in the long run as they do not require ongoing purchases of replacement bags.</p></p>
