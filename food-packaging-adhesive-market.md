<p><strong>The Food Packaging Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-free Adhesive</li><li>Waterborne Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food Packaging Adhesive market offers various types of adhesives based on their composition and application. Solvent adhesives use solvents to disperse the adhesive components, providing a strong bond but with potential environmental risks. Solvent-free adhesives eliminate the use of solvents, reducing health and environmental concerns while maintaining bond strength. Waterborne adhesives use water as a carrier for adhesive components, offering a safe and eco-friendly option with good bonding properties. Each type caters to different needs in the food packaging industry.</p></p>
