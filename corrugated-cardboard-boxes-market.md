<p><p>Corrugated cardboard boxes come in different types based on the number of layers of cardboard used in their construction. Single corrugated boxes have one layer of fluted cardboard sandwiched between two liner boards, offering basic protection. Double corrugated boxes have two layers of fluted cardboard, providing increased strength and durability. Triple corrugated boxes have three layers of fluted cardboard, making them the most robust option for heavy or delicate items. Each type serves a specific purpose in the packaging industry based on the level of protection required.</p></p>
