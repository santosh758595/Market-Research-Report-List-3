<p><p>Passive desktop air purifiers operate by utilizing filters to trap and remove airborne contaminants. Active air purifiers, on the other hand, use technologies like ionization or UV-C light to eliminate pollutants. Hybrid air purifiers combine both passive and active purification methods to provide comprehensive air cleaning. Passive purifiers are known for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while active purifiers are praised for their efficiency in eliminating particles. Hybrid purifiers offer a combination of benefits from both passive and active technologies, providing a comprehensive solution for air purification.</p></p>
