<p><ul><li>Conventional ZLD System</li><li>Hybrid ZLD System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) market consists of two main types of systems: Conventional ZLD Systems and Hybrid ZLD Systems. Conventional ZLD Systems employ a combination of physical and chemical treatment processes to eliminate liquid waste, with the remaining solid waste disposed of in a landfill or used for other purposes. On the other hand, Hybrid ZLD Systems incorporate additional technologies such as membrane filtration or evaporation to further reduce the volume of liquid waste, resulting in improved efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Both systems serve the purpose of achieving zero liquid discharge, minimizing environmental impact, and ensuring compliance with regulations.</p></p>
