<p><p>Cone Beam Imaging Systems are used in the medical field to provide high-quality three-dimensional images of patients. In the standing market, patients stand upright while the imaging system rotates around them, capturing images from various angles. Both setups offer efficient imaging solutions, allowing medical professionals to make accurate diagnoses and treatment plans.</p></p>
