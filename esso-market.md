<p><p>ESSO (Enterprise Single Sign-On) is a type of software that allows users to access various applications and systems using a single set of credentials. The market for ESSO solutions can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premises options. Cloud-based ESSO is hosted on the internet, allowing users to access the software and their applications remotely. On-premises ESSO, on the other hand, is installed and managed on local servers within an organization's network, providing users with direct access to applications within their physical environment. These options offer businesses flexibility in choosing the deployment model that best suits their needs.</p></p>
