<p><p>Plexiglass, also known as acrylic glass, is produced through various market types such as Casting, Injection, Extrusion, and Other markets. Casting involves pouring liquid acrylic into molds to create the desired shape. Injection molding utilizes high pressure to inject molten acrylic into molds. Extrusion involves pushing acrylic pellets through a heated barrel to form continuous sheets or shapes. Other market types may involve different processes like compression molding or thermoforming. Each market type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
