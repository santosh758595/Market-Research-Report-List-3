<p><p>Quad Flat No-leads (QFN) Package Market Types include Air-cavity QFN and Plastic-moulded QFN. </p><p>Air-cavity QFN packages have a hollow cavity underneath the chip, allowing for better thermal performance and reduced stress on the solder joints. These packages are commonly used in high-power applications where heat dissipation is crucial.</p><p>On the other hand, Plastic-moulded QFN packages have a solid plastic body, making them more cost-effective and easier to manufacture. These packages are often used in consumer electronics and other low to medium-power applications.</p></p>
