STR Analysis examines DNA sequences to confirm cell line identity, while SNP Analysis identifies variations in a single nucleotide within a DNA sequence. Other methods may include karyotyping, isoenzyme analysis, or mitochondrial DNA sequencing. These tests are crucial in verifying the authenticity and purity of cell lines, ensuring reliable research results and drug development processes.</p></p>
