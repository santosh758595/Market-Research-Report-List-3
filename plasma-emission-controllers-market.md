The 0-200nm market focuses on controlling plasma emissions within the ultraviolet range, essential for applications like lithography and surface modification. The 200-500nm market deals with visible light emissions, crucial for spectroscopy and materials processing. Each market type serves specific industries with unique requirements for controlling plasma emissions.</p></p>
