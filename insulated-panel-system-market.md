<p><p>Insulated Panel System market consists of various types of panels such as PU sandwich panels, EPS sandwich panels, Mineral Wool sandwich panels, and other panels. PU sandwich panels are made of a polyurethane foam core, EPS sandwich panels have expanded polystyrene foam core, and Mineral Wool sandwich panels use mineral wool insulation. These panels are used in various industries for thermal insulation and building construction. Other panels in the market include PIR sandwich panels, XPS sandwich panels, and paper honeycomb sandwich panels, offering different insulation properties and applications.</p></p>
