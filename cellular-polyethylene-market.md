<p><strong>The Cellular Polyethylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physically Cross-linked Polyethylene Foam</li><li>Chemically Cross-linked Polyethylene Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Physically cross-linked polyethylene foam is created through a process that involves the use of high-energy electron beams or gamma rays to link the polymer chains together. This type of foam is known for its excellent compression, impact resistance, and thermal insulation properties. On the other hand, chemically cross-linked polyethylene foam is produced by adding a chemical foaming agent during the manufacturing process, which creates a stronger and more durable foam material. Both types of polyethylene foam are widely used in various industries for packaging, insulation, and cushioning applications.</p></p>
