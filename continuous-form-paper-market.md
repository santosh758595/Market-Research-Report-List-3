<p><strong>The Continuous Form Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single layer continuous form paper refers to paper that is made up of a single layer and is commonly used for simple printing purposes. On the other hand, multi-layer continuous form paper consists of multiple layers that are interleaved to create copies when written or printed on. Both types of paper cater to different markets, with single layer paper being more cost-effective and suitable for basic needs, while multi-layer paper is ideal for applications requiring duplicate or triplicate copies.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Continuous Form Paper Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dot-matrix Printers</li><li>Line Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous form paper is commonly used in dot-matrix printers and line printers. Dot-matrix printers use pins to create characters on the paper by striking an ink-soaked ribbon. They are often used for printing documents such as invoices and shipping labels. Line printers, on the other hand, use a series of printheads to print a whole line of text at once. They are typically used for high-volume printing tasks such as reports and statements. Both types of printers require continuous form paper to function efficiently.</p></p>
