<p><p>Rare Earth Luminescent Materials Market consists of various types including Photoluminescence, Electroluminescence, Radiation Luminescence, Chemiluminescence, Bioluminescence, and Others. Photoluminescence involves the emission of light upon excitation with photons. Electroluminescence refers to light emission due to electrical current passing through a material. Radiation Luminescence involves light emission from radioactive decay. Chemiluminescence is the production of light through a chemical reaction. Bioluminescence is light emission in living organisms. Other types include thermoluminescence, sonoluminescence, and triboluminescence.</p></p>
