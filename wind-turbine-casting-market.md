Cabin Seat is where all turbine controls are housed. Pedestal is the base where the turbine is mounted. Gearbox Castings house the gears that transfer power from blades to generator. Each type plays a vital role in the functioning of a wind turbine, contributing to efficient energy production.</p></p>
