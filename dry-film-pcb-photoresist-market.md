<p><p>Dry film PCB photoresist is used to transfer circuit patterns onto a board during the PCB manufacturing process. There are two main types of dry film photoresist markets: positive and negative. In positive photoresist, the areas exposed to UV light become soluble and are removed, leaving behind the desired circuit pattern. In negative photoresist, the areas protected from UV light become soluble and are removed, revealing the circuit pattern. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the PCB manufacturing process.</p></p>
