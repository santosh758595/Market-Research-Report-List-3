<p><p>The wheeled walkers market is categorized into three types based on the number of wheels: two-wheeled, three-wheeled, and four-wheeled walkers. Two-wheeled walkers have two front wheels, providing stability and maneuverability for users. Three-wheeled walkers have one wheel in the front and two at the back, allowing for better maneuverability in tight spaces. Four-wheeled walkers have two wheels in the front and two at the back, providing the highest level of stability and support, along with easy maneuverability. These different types cater to various mobility needs and preferences of individuals.</p></p>
