Air filters are used to remove dust and debris from the air entering the engine, ensuring efficient combustion. Oil filters remove contaminants from the engine oil, preventing engine wear and damage. Fuel filters prevent impurities from reaching the engine, maintaining fuel quality. These filters play a crucial role in maintaining the performance and longevity of an automotive engine.</p></p>
