<p><p>Polyethylene terephthalate (PET) dome lids are available in two main types: plain dome lids and slotted dome lids. Plain dome lids are solid and closed on the top, providing full coverage and protection for the contents underneath. On the other hand, slotted dome lids have openings or slots on the top, allowing for easy access to the contents without fully removing the lid. Both types of dome lids are commonly used in food packaging and disposable containers for convenience and hygiene purposes.</p></p>
