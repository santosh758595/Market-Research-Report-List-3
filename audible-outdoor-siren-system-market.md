<p><p>Audible outdoor siren systems are available in two main market types: omni directional and rotating directional. Omni directional sirens emit sound in all directions, providing widespread coverage. Rotating directional sirens, on the other hand, can be adjusted to focus on specific areas by rotating the sound output. Both types are commonly used in emergency situations such as natural disasters or civil defense warnings to alert people in the vicinity.</p></p>
