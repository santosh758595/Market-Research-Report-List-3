The healing screw helps facilitate the integration of the implant with the surrounding bone and gums, ensuring a stable and successful implant placement.</p><p>The dental healing screw market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years due to the increasing demand for dental implants worldwide. <p><strong>The Dental Healing Screw Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Titanium</li><li>Zirconium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental healing screw market comprises two main types: titanium and zirconium. Titanium healing screws are widely used in dental implant surgeries due to their biocompatibility, durability, and corrosion resistance properties. They promote osseointegration, the fusion of the implant with the jawbone. On the other hand, zirconium healing screws are gaining popularity for their tooth-colored appearance, making them more aesthetically pleasing. Zirconium is also highly resistant to fractures and offers exceptional biocompatibility. Both titanium and zirconium healing screws contribute to successful dental treatments by providing stability and support to the implant structure.</p></p>
