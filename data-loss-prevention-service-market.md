<p><strong>The Data Loss Prevention Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Network DLP</li><li>Endpoint DLP</li><li>Storage/Data Center DLP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Loss Prevention (DLP) service market can be divided into three main types: Network DLP, Endpoint DLP, and Storage/Data Center DLP. </p><p>Network DLP focuses on preventing data leaks through the network infrastructure, monitoring and controlling data transfers to identify and block any potential breaches. Endpoint DLP focuses on securing individual devices like laptops, smartphones, and tablets, protecting data from unauthorized access, theft, or accidental leakage. Storage/Data Center DLP secures data stored in servers and data centers, employing measures to prevent data breaches, data loss, or unauthorized access within the storage infrastructure.</p></p>
