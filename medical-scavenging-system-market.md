<p><strong>The Medical Scavenging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Vacuum Systems</li><li>Passive Vacuum Systems</li><li>Centralized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Scavenging System Market encompasses various types such as active vacuum systems, passive vacuum systems, and centralized systems. Active vacuum systems utilize a mechanical vacuum pump to remove waste anesthetic gases. Passive vacuum systems rely on negative room pressure to draw out the gases. Centralized systems collect waste gases from multiple treatment areas into a centralized unit for disposal or recirculation. These systems play a crucial role in maintaining a safe environment for healthcare professionals and patients by preventing the accumulation of harmful anesthetic gases.</p></p>
