<p><p>Magneto-Rheological Damper market consists of two main types: Active Damper and Semi-Active Damper. Active dampers adjust the damping force in real-time based on sensors and control algorithms, offering superior performance but requiring more complex control systems. Semi-active dampers provide adjustable damping levels but do not require as much power as active dampers, making them more cost-effective. Both types offer improved ride quality, handling, and stability compared to traditional passive dampers, making them popular choices in the automotive and construction industries.</p></p>
