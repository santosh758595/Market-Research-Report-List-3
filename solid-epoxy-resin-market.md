<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity Epoxy Resin</li><li>Medium Viscosity Epoxy Resin</li><li>High Viscosity Epoxy Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid epoxy resin market is divided into three types based on viscosity: Low viscosity epoxy resin, medium viscosity epoxy resin, and high viscosity epoxy resin. Low viscosity epoxy resin is commonly used for casting, laminating, and coating applications due to its excellent flow properties. Medium viscosity epoxy resin is ideal for adhesive bonding, potting, and encapsulation applications. High viscosity epoxy resin is mainly used for structural bonding and composite manufacturing due to its high strength and durability. Each type of resin caters to specific industry needs based on their viscosity properties.</p></p>
