<p><p>Ultra Fine Aluminium Powder market can be classified into two types: Spherical Aluminum Powder and Non-Spherical Aluminum Powder. Spherical Aluminum Powder is characterized by its round shape, which allows for better flowability and packing density, making it ideal for applications requiring uniform distribution, such as coatings and additives. On the other hand, Non-Spherical Aluminum Powder consists of irregularly shaped particles, providing increased surface area and reactivity, making it suitable for applications like explosives and rocket propellants. Both types offer unique properties for various industrial uses.</p></p>
