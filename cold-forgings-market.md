<p><p>Cold forgings are categorized into three main market types: Custom Forging, Captive Forging, and Catalog Forging. Custom Forging involves producing parts based on specific customer requirements, offering unique and tailor-made solutions. Captive Forging refers to in-house forging operations within a company, ensuring control over quality and costs. Catalog Forging involves producing a standard range of parts that are readily available for purchase by various customers. Each market type offers different advantages and caters to diverse customer needs in the cold forging industry.</p></p>
