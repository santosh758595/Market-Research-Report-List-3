<p><p>Resin-Based Paste Market includes two main types - Epoxy Resin Based and Methacrylate Resin Based. Epoxy resin-based paste is known for its excellent adhesion and chemical resistance, making it suitable for bonding metal, ceramic, and composite materials. On the other hand, Methacrylate resin-based paste offers fast curing times and high flexibility, making it ideal for applications where quick repairs are needed. Both types of resin-based pastes are widely used in construction, automotive, aerospace, and marine industries for various bonding and repair applications.</p></p>
