<p><ul><li>Straight Moving Type</li><li>Pilot Operated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Pressure Solenoid Valve market consists of two main types: Straight Moving Type and Pilot Operated Type. </p><p>Straight Moving Type valves operate by directly opening and closing the valve port, controlled by an electromagnetic coil. Pilot Operated Type valves, on the other hand, use a smaller solenoid to control the flow of a larger fluid, allowing for higher pressures to be controlled more precisely. Both types cater to different applications and industries where high pressure control is essential.</p></p>
