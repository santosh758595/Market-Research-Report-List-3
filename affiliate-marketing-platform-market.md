<p><p>Affiliate marketing platforms offer different market types based on how advertisers pay affiliates. Cost Per Sale (CPS) market type means that affiliates only earn a commission when a sale is made. In Cost Per Lead (CPL) market type, affiliates get paid for generating leads or potential customers. Cost Per Click (CPC) market is where affiliates earn a commission for every click they drive to the advertisers' website. These market types allow advertisers to align their payment structure with the desired outcomes and provide flexibility to affiliates based on their preferred earning method.</p></p>
