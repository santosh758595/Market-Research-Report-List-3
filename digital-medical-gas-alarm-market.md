<p><p>The Digital Medical Gas Alarm market includes three main types of alarms: Master Alarm, Area Alarm, and Local Alarm. Master Alarms monitor an entire medical gas system, providing an overarching alert for any issues. Area Alarms focus on specific sections or zones within a facility, allowing for more localized monitoring. Local Alarms are designed for individual medical gas outlets or devices, providing immediate notification for nearby users. Each type of alarm plays a crucial role in maintaining the safety and functionality of medical gas systems in healthcare settings.</p></p>
