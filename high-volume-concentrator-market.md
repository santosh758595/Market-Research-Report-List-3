<p><p>High volume concentrators are used for air and water purification and come in two main types: Zeolite and Activated Carbon. Zeolite is a naturally occurring mineral with high adsorption capabilities, making it effective for removing toxins and odors from air and water. Activated Carbon, on the other hand, is a processed form of carbon that has been treated to increase its surface area and adsorption capacity. Both types of concentrators are widely used in industrial and residential applications for purifying air and water.</p></p>
