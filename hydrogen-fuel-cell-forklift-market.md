<p><p>The hydrogen fuel cell forklift market can be categorized into three main types: PEMFC forklift, DMFC forklift, and others. PEMFC forklifts use a proton exchange membrane to convert hydrogen into electricity, making them highly efficient and popular in the market. DMFC forklifts utilize a direct methanol fuel cell system, which is advantageous for applications requiring high energy density. The “others” category includes forklifts using different types of hydrogen fuel cell technologies outside of PEMFC and DMFC.</p></p>
