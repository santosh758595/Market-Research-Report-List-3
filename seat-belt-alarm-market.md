<p><p>Seat Belt Alarm market consists of two main types - Wireless Alarm System and Wired Alarm System. Wireless Alarm System uses sensors and transmitters to detect if a seat belt is not buckled and alerts the driver with a sound or visual alarm. This system is convenient and easy to install. On the other hand, Wired Alarm System uses physical connections between the sensors and the alarm, providing a reliable and effective alert system. Both systems aim to increase seat belt usage and improve road safety.</p></p>
