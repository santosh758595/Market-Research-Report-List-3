<p><ul><li>Continuous Electrodialysis</li><li>Batch Electrodialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Electrodialysis System market can be divided into two types: Continuous Electrodialysis and Batch Electrodialysis. Continuous Electrodialysis involves the continuous operation of the system, allowing for a steady flow of ions to be separated from a solution. On the other hand, Batch Electrodialysis operates in cycles, where a certain amount of solution is processed at a time. Both types of systems have their own advantages and are used in various industrial applications for desalination, wastewater treatment, and food processing.</p></p>
