<p><strong>The Ceramic Burners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grid Ceramic Burner</li><li>Sleeve Ceramic Burner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic burners come in two main types: Grid Ceramic Burners and Sleeve Ceramic Burners. Grid Ceramic Burners feature a grid-like surface for evenly distributing heat and are commonly used in outdoor grills and stoves. On the other hand, Sleeve Ceramic Burners have a cylindrical shape with small holes for combustion, providing a more controlled and concentrated flame. They are often used in indoor gas stoves and heaters. Both types of ceramic burners are efficient and durable options for various heating applications.</p></p>
