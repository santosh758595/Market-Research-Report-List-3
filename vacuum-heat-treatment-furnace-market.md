<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Furnace</li><li>Double Chamber Furnace</li><li>Multi-chamber Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum heat treatment furnaces come in three main types: single chamber, double chamber, and multi-chamber. Single chamber furnaces have only one chamber for processing materials. Double chamber furnaces have two chambers for separate heating and cooling processes. Multi-chamber furnaces consist of multiple chambers for different heat treatment processes to be carried out simultaneously. Each type of furnace caters to different industries and applications, offering a range of capabilities and efficiencies to meet varying needs of heat treatment processes.</p></p>
