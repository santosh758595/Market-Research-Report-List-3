<p><p>LTCC 5G mmWave Filters are based on Low Temperature Co-fired Ceramic technology, known for its high performance, durability, and temperature stability. These filters are ideal for high-frequency applications in 5G systems. On the other hand, SAW 5G mmWave Filters are based on Surface Acoustic Wave technology, offering excellent performance in terms of insertion loss, bandwidth, and rejection capabilities. Both LTCC and SAW filters play a crucial role in ensuring efficient signal transmission and reception in 5G mmWave networks.</p></p>
