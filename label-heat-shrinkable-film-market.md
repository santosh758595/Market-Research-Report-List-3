<p><p>Label heat shrinkable films are classified into different types based on the material used in their production. PVC film is preferred for its excellent shrinkage properties, while PET film is known for its superior strength and clarity. OPS film offers high shrinkage rates and is often used for tamper-evident packaging. Other types of label heat shrinkable films include PLA film, which is eco-friendly, and POF film, which provides good sealing and barrier properties. Each type of film caters to specific needs in the labeling industry.</p></p>
