<p><p>Racing sailboats are divided into two main market types: monohull and multihull. Monohull sailboats have a single hull and are known for their traditional design and performance characteristics. They are favored in competitive racing events such as the America's Cup. On the other hand, multihull sailboats have multiple hulls connected by a deck structure. They offer increased stability, speed, and space compared to monohulls. Multihulls are popular in cruising and offshore racing competitions such as the Volvo Ocean Race.</p></p>
