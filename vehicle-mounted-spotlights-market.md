<p><p>The vehicle mounted spotlights market consists of different types of lighting technologies. HID (High-Intensity Discharge) spotlights use xenon gas and electric current to produce a bright white light, offering a longer lifespan than other options. Incandescent spotlights use a heated filament to produce light but have a shorter lifespan. LED (Light-Emitting Diode) spotlights offer energy efficiency and durability. Halogen lamp spotlights emit a bright warm light and are cost-effective. These different types of spotlights cater to various needs and preferences in the vehicle mounted spotlight market.</p></p>
