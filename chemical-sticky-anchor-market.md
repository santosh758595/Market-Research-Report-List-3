<p><ul><li>Injectable Adhensive Anchors</li><li>Capsule Adhensive Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical Sticky Anchor market includes two main types - Injectable Adhesive Anchors and Capsule Adhesive Anchors. Injectable Adhesive Anchors are known for their ease of use and quick installation process. They are commonly used in overhead applications where drilling is difficult. On the other hand, Capsule Adhesive Anchors consist of resin and hardener in a sealed capsule, providing a convenient and efficient anchoring solution. These anchors are preferred for their high bond strength and reliability in high-performance applications.</p></p>
