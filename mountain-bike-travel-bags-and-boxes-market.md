<p><p>Mountain bike travel bags and boxes come in two main types - soft shell and hard shell. Soft shell bags are usually made of durable fabric and offer flexibility in terms of packing and storing the bike. They are lightweight and easier to maneuver, but may not provide as much protection as hard shell boxes. Hard shell boxes, on the other hand, are rigid and offer superior protection against impact and rough handling during travel. They are heavier and bulkier compared to soft shell bags, but ensure the bike remains safe and secure during transportation.</p></p>
