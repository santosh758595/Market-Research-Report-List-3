<p><p>Metal finishing market can be broadly categorized into three types - Inorganic, Organic, and Hybrid metal finishing. Inorganic metal finishing processes involve the use of chemicals such as acids, bases, and salts to provide corrosion resistance and improve appearance. Organic metal finishing techniques commonly use polymers to provide a decorative and protective coating. Hybrid metal finishing combines techniques from both inorganic and organic processes to achieve optimal results. Each type of metal finishing offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the desired outcome for the final product.</p></p>
