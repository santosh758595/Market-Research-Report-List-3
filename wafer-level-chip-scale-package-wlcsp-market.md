<p><p>Wafer Level Chip Scale Package (WLCSP) is a packaging technology used in the semiconductor industry. There are two main market types within the WLCSP market - Wafer Bumping and Shellcase. Wafer Bumping involves adding bumps to the surface of the wafer before dicing, while Shellcase encapsulates the entire die in a protective shell. Both market types offer advantages such as smaller form factors, better thermal performance, and improved signal integrity compared to traditional packaging methods.</p></p>
