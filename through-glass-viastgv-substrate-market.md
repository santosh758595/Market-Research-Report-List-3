<p><p>Through Glass Vias (TGV) Substrate is a type of substrate used in advanced electronics packaging. TGV substrates are made by drilling micro-sized holes through glass wafers using laser technology, allowing for the integration of electrical circuits in a 3D structure. This technology enables the miniaturization and increased performance of electronic devices such as sensors, MEMS devices, RF components and more.</p><p>The TGV substrate market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for compact and high-performance electronic devices is a key driver for the growth of the TGV substrate market. With the growing trend of wearable electronics, IoT devices, and the rise of 5G technology, there is a growing need for advanced packaging solutions such as TGV substrates.</p><p>Additionally, the automotive industry is another key sector driving the demand for TGV substrates, with applications in ADAS, LiDAR sensors, and other automotive electronics. The TGV substrate market is also seeing innovations in materials and manufacturing processes, leading to improved performance and cost-effectiveness for electronic devices. Overall, the TGV substrate market is poised for steady growth in the coming years.</p></p>
