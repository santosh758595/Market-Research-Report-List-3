<p><strong>The Semiconductor Reflow Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Vapour Phase Ovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Convection ovens in the semiconductor reflow oven market use heated air to transfer heat to the components, resulting in a fast and efficient reflow process. Vapour phase ovens, on the other hand, use a special fluid with a low boiling point to create a vapor that transfers heat evenly to the components, reducing the risk of overheating and enabling precise temperature control. Both types of ovens are commonly used in the industry for their reliability and effectiveness in soldering electronic components onto circuit boards.</p></p>
