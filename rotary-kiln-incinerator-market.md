<p><p>Rotary Kiln Incinerator market consists of two main types: Counter Current and Co-Current Rotary Kiln Incinerators. In a Counter Current design, the waste and air flow in opposite directions, maximizing combustion efficiency and reducing emissions. On the other hand, in a Co-Current design, the waste and air flow in the same direction, allowing for higher capacities and faster processing of waste. Both types have their advantages depending on specific needs and requirements of the incineration process.</p></p>
