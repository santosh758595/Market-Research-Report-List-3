<p><strong>The Polyester Strapping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embossed PET Strapping</li><li>Smooth PET Strapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester strapping market consists of two main types - Embossed PET Strapping and Smooth PET Strapping. Embossed PET Strapping is designed with a textured surface to increase friction and prevent slippage, making it ideal for securing heavy loads. On the other hand, Smooth PET Strapping is characterized by its smooth surface finish, providing high tensile strength and resistance to UV rays. Both types are commonly used in various industries for packaging, bundling, and securing goods during transportation.</p></p>
