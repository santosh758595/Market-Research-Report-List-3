<p><p>Optical chip substrate market can be categorized into different types based on the materials used. InP, GaAs, Si, LiNb03, and Others are the main types in this segment. Indium Phosphide (InP) offers high electron mobility and is suitable for optical communications. Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) is commonly used in optoelectronics due to its high electron mobility. Silicon (Si) is preferred for its cost-effectiveness and compatibility with existing manufacturing processes. Lithium Niobate (LiNb03) is known for its piezoelectric properties, making it ideal for optical applications. Other materials used in optical chip substrates include glass, sapphire, and polymers.</p></p>
