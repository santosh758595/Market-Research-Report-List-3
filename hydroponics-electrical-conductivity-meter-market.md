<p><p>The hydroponics electrical conductivity meter market includes two main types: completely waterproof electrical conductivity meters and waterproof probe electrical conductivity meters. Completely waterproof meters are fully sealed and can be submerged in water without damage, making them ideal for use in hydroponic systems. Waterproof probe meters have a waterproof probe that can be immersed in water while the main unit remains dry, providing a more versatile option for measuring electrical conductivity in different settings. Both types are essential tools for monitoring and maintaining nutrient levels in hydroponic systems.</p></p>
