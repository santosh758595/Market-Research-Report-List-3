<p><strong>The Paste PVC (PPVC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homogeneous Type</li><li>Heterogeneous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paste PVC (PPVC) can be classified into two main market types: homogeneous type and heterogeneous type. Homogeneous type PPVC is characterized by consistent particle size and distribution, resulting in a uniform appearance and performance. Heterogeneous type PPVC, on the other hand, contains varying particle sizes and distribution, leading to a more diverse and sometimes uneven finish. Both types cater to different market demands, with homogeneous type favored for applications requiring a consistent finish, and heterogeneous type preferred for projects where a more varied aesthetic is desired.</p></p>
