<p><p>Metal cleaners market can be categorized into two main types - alkaline cleaners and acid cleaners. Alkaline cleaners are effective in removing organic contaminants such as oils, greases, and carbon deposits from metal surfaces. They are commonly used in industrial applications due to their strong cleaning capabilities. On the other hand, acid cleaners are suitable for removing inorganic contaminants like rust, scale, and oxidation from metal surfaces. Each type of cleaner has its own specific uses and advantages depending on the cleaning requirements of the metal surface.</p></p>
