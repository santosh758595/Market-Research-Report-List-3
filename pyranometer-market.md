<p><p>Pyranometers are instruments used to measure solar radiation levels. There are two main types of pyranometers in the market: thermopile pyranometers and photovoltaic pyranometers. Thermopile pyranometers use a thermopile sensor to measure the heat generated by solar radiation, while photovoltaic pyranometers use a photovoltaic cell to convert solar radiation directly into electricity for measurement. Both types are commonly used in various applications such as meteorology, solar energy research, environmental monitoring, and agriculture.</p></p>
