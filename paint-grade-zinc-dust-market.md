<p><strong>The Paint Grade Zinc Dust Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Atomization Method</li><li>Hydraulic Atomization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paint Grade Zinc Dust is used in various industries for corrosion protection and as a pigment in paints and coatings. The market can be categorized into two types based on the production method: Air Atomization Method and Hydraulic Atomization Method. Air Atomization Method involves spraying molten zinc into a high-velocity stream of air, resulting in fine particles suitable for paint applications. Hydraulic Atomization Method, on the other hand, uses a high-pressure liquid stream to break up molten zinc into smaller particles, also ideal for paint grade applications.</p></p>
