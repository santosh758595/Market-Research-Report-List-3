<p><strong>The Centrifugal Water Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Overhung Impeller</li><li>Vertically Suspended</li><li>Between Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal water pumps are classified into three main types based on the location of the impeller within the pump. Overhung impeller pumps have the impeller mounted on the end of the shaft, vertically suspended pumps have the impeller mounted in the middle of the pump casing, and between bearing pumps have the impeller mounted between two bearings. Each type of pump is designed for specific applications and operating conditions, offering different advantages and performance characteristics.</p></p>
