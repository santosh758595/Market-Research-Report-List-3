<p><p>Wafer Packaging Inspection System market is categorized into two main types: Optical Based and Infrared Type. </p><p>Optical Based systems use visible light to analyze the surface quality, defects, and dimensions of wafers during the packaging process. Infrared Type systems, on the other hand, utilize infrared radiation to detect variations in temperature and identify potential issues like delamination or moisture absorption. Both types of systems play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor wafers in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
