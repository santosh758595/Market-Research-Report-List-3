<p><p>The baggage carousel market can be categorized into single-level and multi-level systems. Single-level carousels are simpler and typically found in smaller airports, where bags are loaded onto a single rotating belt for passengers to retrieve. In contrast, multi-level carousels consist of multiple levels of rotating belts that allow for more efficient baggage handling in larger airports with higher passenger traffic. Multi-level systems can accommodate a larger volume of bags and prevent overcrowding on the carousel area.</p></p>
