<p><p>Screw Plug Heaters come in different market types, including Mechanical Thermostat, Thermocouple, and RTD Sensor. Mechanical Thermostat screw plug heaters consist of a simple mechanical device that regulates the temperature. Thermocouple screw plug heaters use thermocouple probes to measure temperature and provide accurate readings. RTD Sensor screw plug heaters utilize resistance temperature detectors to measure temperature. Each market type offers different features and benefits, catering to various applications and requirements. These screw plug heaters play a crucial role in maintaining temperature in industrial processes effectively.</p></p>
