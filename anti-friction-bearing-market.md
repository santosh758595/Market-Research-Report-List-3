<p><p>The anti-friction bearing market consists of various types, including ball bearings, roller bearings, and other types. Roller bearings, on the other hand, use cylindrical or tapered rollers to reduce friction and support heavy loads. The other segment encompasses less common designs such as thrust bearings, angular contact bearings, and needle bearings. All these bearing types contribute to reducing friction and enhancing the performance and durability of various machinery and equipment.</p></p>
