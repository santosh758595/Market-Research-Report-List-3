<p><strong>The Graphene-Based Supercapacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrical Double Layer Capacitors</li><li>Pseudocapacitor</li><li>Asymmetric Supercapacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphene-Based Supercapacitors can be categorized into three main types - Electrical Double Layer Capacitors, Pseudocapacitors, and Asymmetric Supercapacitors. Electrical Double Layer Capacitors store energy through the separation of charge at the interface between the electrode and electrolyte. Pseudocapacitors use redox reactions to store energy, allowing for higher capacitance. Asymmetric Supercapacitors combine the features of both Electrical Double Layer Capacitors and Pseudocapacitors to achieve higher energy density and power density. Each type offers unique advantages for different applications in the supercapacitor market.</p></p>
