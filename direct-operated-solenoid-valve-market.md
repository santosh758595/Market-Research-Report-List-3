<p><p>Direct-operated solenoid valves come in various types based on the number of ports they have: 2-way, 3-way, 4-way, and 5-way. A 2-way solenoid valve has two ports for on/off control of fluid flow. A 3-way solenoid valve has three ports for switching between two different paths. A 4-way solenoid valve has four ports for controlling double-acting cylinders. A 5-way solenoid valve has five ports for more complex operations. Other types of direct-operated solenoid valves cater to specific industry needs.</p></p>
