<p><p>Aggregate concrete is commonly classified into two types based on the materials used: natural materials aggregate concrete and by-products aggregate concrete. </p><p>Natural materials aggregate concrete is made from locally sourced natural materials such as sand, gravel, and crushed stone. These materials are commonly used in construction due to their availability and durability.</p><p>By-products aggregate concrete, on the other hand, is made using recycled materials such as crushed concrete, glass, and slag. This type of concrete is sustainable and helps reduce the environmental impact of construction projects.</p></p>
