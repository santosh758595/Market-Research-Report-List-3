<p><p>ESD Anti-Static Eliminators are devices designed to eliminate static electricity in various industries. Contact Type Static Eliminators work by physically contacting the charged surface to neutralize the charge, while Induction Type Static Eliminators generate ions that neutralize the static charge without physical contact. Contact Type Static Eliminators are suitable for smaller areas and direct contact with surfaces, while Induction Type Static Eliminators are ideal for larger areas and remote charging points. Both types play a crucial role in preventing damage caused by static electricity.</p></p>
