<p><p>Volume Bragg Grating (VBG) market includes several types such as Reflective Bragg Grating (RBG), Transmitive Bragg Grating (TBG), Combined Bragg Grating (CBG) and Binary Nanoparticle Film (BNF). RBG is used for reflective applications, while TBG is used for transmission applications. CBG combines RBG and TBG for both reflective and transmission applications. BNF involves binary nanoparticles for various applications. Each type of VBG caters to specific requirements and offers unique benefits in terms of efficiency, precision, and performance in optical systems.</p></p>
