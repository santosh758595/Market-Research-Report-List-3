<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Dual Sensors</li><li>Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial smoke detectors come in different types to cater to various needs: Photoelectric smoke detectors use light beams to detect smoke particles, making them suitable for slow, smoldering fires. Dual sensor smoke detectors combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies for comprehensive fire detection. Ionization smoke detectors are more sensitive to fast flaming fires. Each type has its advantages and is suitable for different types of fires, making it essential for commercial buildings to have a combination of these detectors for optimal fire safety.</p></p>
