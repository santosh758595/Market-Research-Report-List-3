<p><p>Electronic Stability Control (ESC) system is a safety feature installed in vehicles to improve stability, handling, and traction. It helps in reducing the risk of accidents by automatically applying brakes to individual wheels and adjusting engine torque to maintain the intended path of the vehicle. ESC systems monitor various parameters such as steering angle, rotational speed, and lateral acceleration, and intervene when the vehicle is on the verge of losing control. It is widely used in passenger cars, commercial vehicles, and sports utility vehicles for enhanced safety and control.</p><p>The global Electronic Stability Control System market has been witnessing significant growth due to the increasing adoption of safety systems in vehicles. The implementation of stringent government regulations and the rising demand for safe and secure vehicles are expected to drive the market growth. Additionally, the growing consumer awareness regarding the advantages of ESC systems and the increasing investment by automakers in research and development activities further contribute to market growth.</p><p>The market is witnessing several trends that are shaping its growth.
