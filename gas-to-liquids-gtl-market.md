One of the key drivers is the increasing demand for clean and sustainable fuels, as GTL fuels have lower emissions compared to conventional fuels. One of the major trends is the development of small-scale GTL plants, which provide an opportunity to monetize stranded or flared gas reserves that are not economically viable to transport or use otherwise. These small-scale plants offer flexibility, shorter lead times, and reduced capital costs compared to large-scale GTL plants.</p><p>Another trend is the integration of GTL technology with renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power, to produce renewable synthetic fuels.
