<p><p>Rockwool roof insulation is commonly used in both pitch roof and flat roof applications. In pitch roof insulation, Rockwool boards or batts are installed between roof rafters to minimize heat loss and improve energy efficiency. In flat roof insulation, Rockwool insulation is laid on top of the roof deck to provide thermal protection and prevent moisture buildup. Both applications help regulate indoor temperatures, reduce energy consumption, and enhance overall building performance.</p></p>
