<p><ul><li>Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) Systems</li><li>Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry etch systems are widely used in semiconductor manufacturing for patterning and etching processes. The two main types of dry etch systems are Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) Systems and Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) Systems. RIE systems use chemically reactive gases to etch materials, while DRIE systems are specifically designed for etching deep, high aspect ratio features. Both types of systems play a crucial role in achieving precise and complex etching patterns in semiconductor devices and microelectronics.</p></p>
