<p><strong>The Magnetorheological Damper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Damper</li><li>Semi Active Damper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active dampers employ an external power source to actively adjust damping force, providing real-time response to changing road conditions. They offer the highest level of control but also tend to be more complex and expensive. Semi-active dampers, on the other hand, utilize a combination of passive and active components to adjust damping force based on input from sensors. They offer a good balance between performance and cost, making them popular in the automotive industry. Both types of dampers enhance vehicle stability and control, providing a smoother and safer driving experience.</p></p>
