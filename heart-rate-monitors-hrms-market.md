<p><strong>The Heart Rate Monitors (HRMs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chest-strap HRMs</li><li>Wrist-based HRMs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heart Rate Monitors (HRMs) come in two main types: Chest-strap HRMs and Wrist-based HRMs. Chest-strap HRMs are worn around the chest and provide accurate heart rate measurements by detecting electrical signals from the heart. On the other hand, Wrist-based HRMs are worn on the wrist and use optical sensors to measure heart rate. Both types of HRMs cater to different preferences and usage scenarios, with chest-strap HRMs known for their accuracy and wrist-based HRMs for their convenience and comfort.</p></p>
