<p><p>Co-processed excipients market types include granulation, spray drying, hot melt extrusion, solvent evaporation, and others. Granulation involves blending and compacting multiple ingredients into granules. Spray drying converts liquid formulations into powders through a drying process. Hot melt extrusion mixes ingredients and melts them into a uniform mixture for easy processing. Solvent evaporation removes solvents from a solution to form a solid material. Other methods may include combinations of these techniques or alternative processes for creating co-processed excipients.</p></p>
