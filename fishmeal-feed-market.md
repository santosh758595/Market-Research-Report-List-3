<p><strong>The Fishmeal Feed Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Dried(SD)</li><li>Flame Dried(FD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fishmeal feed market types refer to the different drying methods used in the production process. Steam dried (SD) fishmeal is made by exposing fish to high-pressure steam, while flame dried (FD) fishmeal is created by flame drying the fish. SD fishmeal tends to have a higher protein content and is lighter in color, while FD fishmeal is darker in color and has a stronger flavor. Both types of fishmeal are commonly used in animal feed due to their high protein content and nutritional value.</p></p>
